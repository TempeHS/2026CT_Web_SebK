Minigames Guide

In Cookie Clicker there are 4 different minigames

1. The Garden

In the Garden you can grow many different plants, each one giving different effects.



2. The Stock Market

In the Stock Market you can invest in different ingriedents to potentially gain money.



3. The Pantheon

In the Pantheon you can choose to worship 3 different cookie gods, each providing an effect.



4. The Grimoire

In the Grimoire you can use mana to cast spells to gain effects and start golden cookie combos.
