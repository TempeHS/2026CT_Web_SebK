Golden Cookie Combos Guide

A Golden Cookie is an interactible that appears sometimes. When the player clicks on the cookie they gain a power up.

A Golden Cookie Combo is when multiple golden cookies provide effects in tandem, and multiply the player's cookie count.

Common early game combonations are

  • Frenzy + Click Frenzy (Giving 5439x the regular click power)
  • Frenzy + Any Building Boost (Giving 7x the amount of the building times 100)
